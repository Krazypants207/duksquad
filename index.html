About Us:

The Duk Squad (misspelled on purpose) originally started in 2016. It all started with a game called Terraria. There was an Easter egg where a duck would quack like a human. Our founder, PrincePorky1991, is also known as TEG, or The Endermite Guardian. Porky made a Roblox group and called it the Duk Squad, and the Duk Squad as we know it was born. But, that's not the whole story; the Duk Squad got bigger, much bigger. So, as a means of communication between all the members, the Duk Squad Discord Server was made. It didn't catch on, not many people joined the Discord Server, but we're still updating it and responding to questions. We also post when we come out with new videos on our YouTube channel! A link to join will be provided in the "LOCATIONS" section.
